My Hobbies

A hobby is an activity, interest, or pastime that is undertaken for pleasure or relaxation, done during one's own time. You can pursue more than one hobby too. Music, dance, reading, story writing, sports, philately, painting and theatre may all be hobbies. For instance, one could be a professional flautist, but for another playing the flute cloud be a hobby. A hobby is a fun activity that is pursued during one's leisure hours.
